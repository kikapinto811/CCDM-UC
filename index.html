“Design+Multimedia Conversation Cycle” is a cycle of conferences that takes place annually within the scope of the Bachelor's and Master's courses in Design and Multimedia at the Faculty of Science and Technology of the University of Coimbra. Which will take place in April 2024, divided into four sessions that will take place on Wednesday afternoons, in the auditorium of the Science Museum in Coimbra. Conferences are open to the general public and are free to attend.

Bráulio Amado


Bráulio Amado is a graphic designer and illustrator. Born in 1987 in Almada, Lisbon, and currently lives in New York.
He has worked at Pentagram, Bloomberg Businessweek, Wieden Kennedy, and now runs his own studio, working for clients such as Nike, Redbull Music Academy, The New York Times, Wired Magazine, Beck and Good Room.
In 2015 he received the Young Guns Award from the Art Directors Club of New York, and his work has been awarded by the Type Directors Club and the American Illustration Association.
He has presented exhibitions in New York, Chicago, Portland, Berlin, Lisbon, Madrid, Rome and Ghent.

Camila Nogueira


My name is Camila Nogueira and I’m a Portuguese artist currently living in the beautiful Porto city, Portugal. I graduated in 2015, and ever since I’ve been working as a freelancer developing several commercial illustration projects, as well as making time for my own personal work.
At the moment I’m mainly focused on reinterpreting places with a magical vibe, and creating surreal worlds that I would love to live in. Life is a beautiful mystery, but it has a lot of tough moments. With my art, I seek to take people to the beauty side, making them look at life with a more dreamy lens!
Regarding freelance commissions or work collaborations, please feel free to contact me. I would love to hear from you.

Susana Carvalho


Hi, my name is Susana. In 2005 my partner Kai Bernau and I co-founded Carvalho Bernau. We design publications, identities, typefaces etc. After 18 years in The Hague, we moved to Porto in 2021.
Atelier Carvalho Bernau is a design studio based in Porto, Portugal. The atelier was originally established in 2005 by Susana Carvalho and Kai Bernau in The Hague, the Netherlands. We mainly work for the culture and publishing fields, both locally and internationally.

Rejane dal Bello


We are a graphic design studio, based in London. We specialize in brand identity, illustration, and editorial for national and international clients focusing on the Corporate, Cultural & Non-Profit sectors. Our studio works with ongoing collaborations to best suit each project we work on.
Founded by Rejane Dal Bello, an awarded winning Graphic Designer & Illustrator with a great range of iconic design case studies. Originally from Brazil, she began her career working for renowned branding & design over the world, Studio Dumbar (NL) and Wolff Olins (UK).
